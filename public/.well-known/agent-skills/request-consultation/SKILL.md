# Skill: Request Consultation

Request a free consultation with AI Security about AI automation or cybersecurity services for your business.

## Description
AI Security offers free 30-minute consultations to assess your business needs in AI automation (chatbots, document management, process automation) or cybersecurity (Wazuh, ENS compliance).

## Parameters
- `service` (optional, string): Service of interest. Values: `chatbot`, `gestor-documental`, `gestor-citas`, `atencion-llamadas`, `automatizacion`, `wazuh`, `soporte-tecnico`
- `name` (optional, string): Your name
- `company` (optional, string): Your company name

## How to use
Navigate to https://aisecurity.es/reunion and fill out the consultation request form. A consultant will respond within 24 business hours.

## Returns
A confirmation that the consultation request form is available at https://aisecurity.es/reunion
